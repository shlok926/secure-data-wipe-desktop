# Contributor Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. 
We expect all participants to abide by this code of conduct.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Reporting security vulnerabilities responsibly

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment of any kind
- Discrimination based on protected characteristics
- Offensive comments related to personal characteristics
- Threats or intimidation
- Sharing others' private information without consent
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying the standards of acceptable 
behavior and enforcing these standards fairly.

Community leaders will take action in response to behavior that violates this 
code of conduct, including:

- Warnings
- Temporary bans from participation
- Permanent removal from the community

## Reporting

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be 
reported to the project maintainers at: conduct@securewipe.com

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated. We are committed to maintaining 
the privacy of reporters.
